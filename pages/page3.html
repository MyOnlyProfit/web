Key Metrics (KPI)

Core Metrics to Track

  • Traffic: Profile clicks from TikTok/IG/Reddit. Target: hundreds of link clicks/month
  • Conversion Rate: ~3–5% of link clicks → subscribers. 2,000 visitors × 5% = 100 subs
  • Subscriber Count: New subs per week + retention (how many renew)
  • ARPU: Subscription + PPV + tips. 100 subs @ €15 ≈ €1,200/month net
  • PPV Purchase Rate: ~5–10% of active subscribers per drop
  • DM Response Rate: Target >40% reply rate on personalized messages
