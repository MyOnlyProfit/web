03 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity — to fulfil your purchase and deliver your product
  • Legitimate interests — to respond to enquiries, prevent fraud, and improve our services
  • Consent — where you have explicitly agreed (e.g. ticking the checkbox on our contact form or subscribing to our newsletter)

04 How We Use Your Data

  • To send your product access password after purchase
  • To respond to your contact form submission
  • To assess and respond to Empire™ programme applications
  • To conduct the free profile analysis you requested
  • To communicate relevant updates about your order or access
  • To send newsletters and marketing updates

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

05 Third Parties

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Stripe, Inc. — Payment processing. Stripe handles all card data securely and in compliance with PCI-DSS. We do not receive or store your full card number. See stripe.com/privacy.
  • Vercel — Website hosting and contact form API. Your form submissions pass through Vercel's infrastructure.
  • Microsoft Clarity — Behavioural analytics. See section 08 below for full details.
  • Email service — Used to send automated password delivery emails after purchase.

06 Data Retention

  • Contact form enquiries are retained for up to 12 months or until the matter is resolved
  • Purchase records (email, order details) are retained for 7 years for accounting and legal compliance
  • Payment data is retained by Stripe according to their own retention policy
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Your Rights below)

07 Your Rights

Under GDPR (and applicable Finnish data protection law), you have the following rights:

Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you

Correction

Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data

Deletion

Request erasure of your data where there is no legal obligation to retain it

Restriction

Ask us to limit how we process your data in certain circumstances

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

Objection

Object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email us at system@myonlyprofit.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi).

08 Microsoft Clarity

We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors interact with our website. Clarity records anonymised session replays, generates heatmaps, and tracks behavioural metrics such as clicks, scrolls, and navigation paths — helping us improve site performance and user experience.

Clarity may use first and third-party cookies and collect data including your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and on-site interactions. This data is processed by Microsoft and is subject to the Microsoft Privacy Statement.

We also use Microsoft Advertising in conjunction with Clarity to measure campaign performance and support site optimisation. You can opt out of personalised advertising via your Microsoft privacy settings.

09 Email Marketing

When you submit our contact form and tick the consent checkbox, you agree to be added to our mailing list. We may send you occasional emails with updates, insights, and relevant information related to our services. You can unsubscribe at any time by replying "unsubscribe" to any email or by contacting us at system@myonlyprofit.com. We will never share your email with third parties for marketing purposes.

10 Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies beyond those described in section 08. Third-party services (Stripe, Vercel, Microsoft Clarity) may set their own cookies — please refer to their respective privacy policies for full details.

11 Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data. All data transmitted to our site is encrypted via HTTPS. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe and is never stored on our systems.

12 Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

13 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be published at myonlyprofit.com/privacy.html with a revised date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
